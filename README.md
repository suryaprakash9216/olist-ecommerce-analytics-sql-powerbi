# Olist E-Commerce Analytics | SQL & Power BI

## Project Overview

This project analyses the Olist Brazilian E-Commerce dataset to understand customer behaviour, sales performance, retention trends, and product performance using SQL and Power BI.

The objective was to transform raw transactional data into meaningful business insights through data cleaning, exploratory analysis, customer segmentation, retention analysis, customer lifetime value modelling, and interactive dashboard development.

---

## Business Objectives

* Analyse sales and revenue performance across products and categories.
* Identify high-value customer segments.
* Measure customer retention and repeat purchase behaviour.
* Estimate Customer Lifetime Value (CLV).
* Discover frequently purchased product combinations.
* Build interactive dashboards for business reporting and performance monitoring.

---

## Dataset

The analysis was conducted using the Olist Brazilian E-Commerce dataset containing:

* 99,000+ Orders
* 96,000+ Customers
* 32,000+ Products
* Seller Information
* Customer Reviews
* Payment Data
* Delivery Information

## Project Workflow

### 1. Data Loading

Imported and structured multiple datasets into a relational database environment.

### 2. Data Understanding

Explored table relationships, business entities, and data structures.

### 3. Data Cleaning & Validation

Handled missing values, validated records, standardised formats, and improved data quality.

### 4. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Analysed revenue trends, customer activity, product performance, delivery metrics, and business patterns.

### 5. Customer Segmentation (RFM Analysis)

Segmented customers based on Recency, Frequency, and Monetary value to identify:

* Champions
* Loyal Customers
* At-Risk Customers
* Potential Loyalists

### 6. Cohort Retention Analysis

Evaluated customer retention behaviour across multiple acquisition cohorts.

### 7. Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) Analysis

Estimated long-term customer value and identified high-value customer groups.

### 8. Market Basket Analysis

Identified frequently purchased product combinations and cross-selling opportunities.

### 9. Dashboard Development

Developed interactive Power BI dashboards for business reporting and performance monitoring.

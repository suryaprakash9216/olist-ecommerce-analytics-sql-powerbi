# Olist E-Commerce Analytics | SQL & Power BI

## Project Overview

This project analyses the Olist Brazilian E-Commerce dataset to understand customer behaviour, sales performance, product performance, customer retention, and business growth opportunities using SQL and Power BI.

The project follows a complete analytics workflow, including data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), customer segmentation, retention analysis, customer lifetime value (CLV) analysis, market basket analysis, and interactive dashboard development.

---

## Business Objectives

* Analyse sales and revenue performance across products and categories.
* Identify high-value customer segments.
* Evaluate customer retention and repeat purchase behaviour.
* Estimate Customer Lifetime Value (CLV).
* Discover cross-selling opportunities through Market Basket Analysis.
* Develop interactive dashboards for business reporting and performance monitoring.

---

## Dataset Summary

The Olist Brazilian E-Commerce dataset contains:

* 99K+ Orders
* 96K+ Customers
* 32K+ Products
* Seller Information
* Customer Reviews
* Payment Data
* Delivery & Logistics Data
* Geolocation Data

## Project Workflow

### 1. Data Loading

Imported and structured multiple datasets into a relational database environment.

### 2. Data Understanding

Explored data structures, relationships, and business entities.

### 3. Data Cleaning & Validation

Handled missing values, validated records, standardised formats, and improved data quality.

### 4. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Analysed revenue trends, customer behaviour, product performance, review scores, and logistics performance.

### 5. Customer Segmentation (RFM Analysis)

Segmented customers based on Recency, Frequency, and Monetary Value to identify:

* Champions
* Loyal Customers
* Potential Loyalists
* At-Risk Customers

### 6. Cohort Retention Analysis

Measured customer retention behaviour across acquisition cohorts.

### 7. Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) Analysis

Estimated long-term customer value and identified high-value customer groups.

### 8. Market Basket Analysis

Identified frequently purchased product combinations and potential cross-selling opportunities.

### 9. Dashboard Development

Built interactive Power BI dashboards for business reporting and performance monitoring.
